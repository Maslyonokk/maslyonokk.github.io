I am Alexandru Vasilachi and I am a starting UX designer. I enjoy solving practical problems through design as well as understanding the perspective of users.

I believe the best design allows users to complete their tasks seamlessly and efficiently, while not being noticeable. This is why my design process always involves extensive user research and empathizing, as well as prototyping and iterating to ensure that the end product is functional and enjoyable to use.

Creating designs that are responsive and helpful for important tasks is my biggest interest in UX design.
